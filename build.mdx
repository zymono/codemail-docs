## The Basics

- Create items by dragging from left sidebar
- Delete items with `Shift + Click`
- Edit items with `right click`

## Sidebar

Sidebar provides a way to edit item's text, classes, hrefs, and styles. Simply right click on an item of your choice and edit on the right.
